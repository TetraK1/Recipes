1 kg mince
3 eggs
1 handful parsley, chopped
0.5 clove garlic
0.25 cup bread crumbs
0.5 cup parmesan, grated
1 onion, diced

- In a bowl add mince, eggs, parsley, garlic, bread crumbs, cheese
- mix thoroughly
- form into balls
- cook in sauce, adding no more than 4 at a time
- simmer for half hour once all meatballs are in
